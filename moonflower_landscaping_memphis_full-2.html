Our Story

Moonflower Landscaping was built on one simple belief: every property — commercial or residential — deserves to look its absolute best. We started right here in Memphis, Tennessee, growing from a passion for outdoor spaces into a full-service landscaping company trusted by property managers and homeowners across the region.

We understand that your lawn and landscape are extensions of who you are. For business owners, it's your first impression. For homeowners, it's your sanctuary. That's why we bring the same standard of excellence to every property, every visit.

From Shelby County office parks to Southaven neighborhoods, Moonflower is the team Memphis trusts to show up, work hard, and deliver results you'll be proud of.
